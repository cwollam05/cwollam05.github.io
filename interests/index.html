Beyond the work

My Interests

The things that inspire me and shape how I think creatively.

There's a lot more to me than just design. I'm competitive, creative, and always doing something. Here's a look at what I'm passionate about outside of work!

🎨

Graphic Design

I genuinely love design. I'm always looking at what other people are creating, whether it's a logo, a poster, or a brand identity, and thinking about what makes it work. It never really feels like work to me.

🎬

Filmmaking & Video

Video is something I've been into for a while now. I like the whole process of it, shooting, editing, putting music to it and watching it come together. There's nothing better than finishing a video and actually being proud of how it turned out.

💻

Web & Tech

I really enjoy building things for the web. Getting a design out of my head and into a browser that actually works and looks good is a pretty satisfying feeling. I'm always picking up new things and trying to get better at it.

🎵

Music

Music is always on for me. It sets the mood for pretty much everything I do. I'm into a lot of different stuff and I'm always finding new artists or songs I can't stop listening to.

⚽

Soccer

Soccer has been a part of my life for as long as I can remember. I've played my whole life and I've also coached, so I see the game from both sides. It's competitive, it's fun, and it's one of those things I'll always make time for.

🤠

Line Dancing

Line dancing is something I picked up not too long ago and got hooked on pretty fast. It's a great time, you're always learning new dances, and the people you meet doing it make it even better.
