About Explorlando
Explorlando is a community based platform designed for students, visitors, local residents/organizations, and anyone located in Central Florida. Explorlando was designed with the goal to make everyday needs easily accessible, such as finding nearby events, planning date nights,food spots, other attractions to plan your weekend trips! The purpose is to create a more connected experience for the Central Florida community!
