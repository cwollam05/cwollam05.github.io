About Me

Designer, creator, and Central Florida native.

Hi, I'm Connery!

I'm a Digital Media Designer born and raised in Oviedo, Florida. Growing up in Central Florida really shaped how I see and think about design, and over the years I've built up skills in graphic design, video editing, and web design, three areas I'm genuinely passionate about and love working in.

What keeps me going is wanting to turn ideas into something real. I don't just want to make things that look good, I want to create something that didn't exist before and see it come to life exactly the way I pictured it.

Outside of design, God comes first in everything I do. I volunteer at events through my church and go on mission trips to serve people in countries around the world. I also travel a lot and love getting to experience new places. When I'm not doing that, you'll probably find me on a soccer field, coaching or playing, or behind the wheel of my Mustang or riding my motorcycle.
